What are Guinea Pigs? 

Guinea pigs are small rodent mammals that originated from South America. Fun fact, despite their name they do not originate from Guinea and it is unknown why that is their name. The second part comes from the fact that they have a very similar anatomy to a pig. 

Since being introduced to the Western world they have been popularised as a domesticated pet. 

They are incredibly well loved and very popular pets with families all over the world.
