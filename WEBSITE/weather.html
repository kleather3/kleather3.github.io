How to take care of your Guinea Pig

 • The average guinea pig lives between 4-8 years 

 • They need a decent amount of space, a cage at least somewhere between 70 and 105cm 

 • They are fairly large starting off at about the size of a hamster at birth and then growing to around 30 com in length by adulthood 

 • It is important to remember that guinea pigs do not clean up or keep a super tidy environment, so you need to clean their cage every day and clean them at least once a week 

  • What You Need To Do Every Day: 

       Top up their food, snacks, and hay 

       Change their drinking water and clean out the water bottles 

       Spot clean out where they have been to the bathroom 

       Spend time with them and play with them 

       Exercise! 30 minutes or so is good cause if they don’t exercise they can get fat!! 

• What You Need To Do Weekly: 

       Clean out the whole cage and replace all the shavings and bedding 

       Cut their nails 

       Wash them!
